ALUPA FOODS PRIVATE LIMITED, based in Udupi, Karnataka, has appointed MALLYA & MALLYA as its auditor for a five-year term from April 1, 2022, to March 31, 2027. The appointment was confirmed during the Annual General Meeting on September 26, 2022, following six years of service by the previous auditor. Key documents, including the auditor's written consent and a resolution from the board, were submitted to formalize the appointment. The process was authorized by Director Krishna Kumar Rao, ensuring compliance with relevant corporate regulations.
