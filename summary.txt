The company Alupa Foods Private Limited has appointed the auditing firm Mallya & Mallya for a five-year term, starting from April 1, 2022, and concluding on March 31, 2027. This appointment was officially made during the Annual General Meeting (AGM) on September 26, 2022. The necessary consents and a resolution from the board were documented and attached to the filing, ensuring compliance with the Companies Act, 2013. The declaration confirms that all provided information is accurate and complete, as stated by the company's director, Krishna Kumar Rao.
